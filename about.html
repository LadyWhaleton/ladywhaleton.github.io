About

Bio

Before I learned how to code, I loved to edit videos and draw monsters on my free time. In 2004, when I was 11, I had my first taste of programming by customizing webpages. On a virtual pet website called Neopets. But, ever since then, I realized that I love the idea of creating something from nothing.

When I started college in 2011, I majored in Biological Science, which had little to do with what I loved. Eventually, I realized I was unhappy with that path, and I decided to make the switch to Computer Science in 2013. In the following years, I've learned so much more about programming and couldn't have felt more at home. No regrets! :)
